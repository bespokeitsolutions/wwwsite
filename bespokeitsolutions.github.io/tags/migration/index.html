in blog

Considering migratinon of infrastructure to cloud? Why you should consider migrating to a cloud provider? There are reasons like cost effectivty, you can reduce the cost of hosting your own server. Benefits like high-availability, scaling and auto-scaling. You avoid additonal costs for hardware, you can easily upscale or downscale your cloud servers based on your needs. What are caveats of migration to cloud provider? You don’t know where are your data in terms of physical location, you just believe they are somewhere.
